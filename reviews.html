OUR TOP ALBUMS



DAMN

Kendrick Lamar

Released on April 14, 2017, "DAMN" is Kendrick Lamar's fourth studio album.The album's genre is listed as consious hip-hop but is genre-defying as it incorporates elements from other genres such as jazz, R&B and rock. Kendrick's storytelling and lyrical prowess are at full display on this album. With features from prominent artists such as beautiful vocals from Rihanna and production contributions from James Blake and The Alchemist, there should be no questions asked on why this album is rated so highly. With it's crowning achievment of winning the Pulitzer prize for music in 2018, this album has cemented it as one of the greatest hip-hop albums to ever be created.


Utopia

Travis Scott

Released on July 28, 2023, "Utopia" is Travis Scott's fourth studio album. The album's genre is listed as hip-hop and its sub-genre trap. The album incorporates elements from many other genres as it uses it feature list to create diverse sounds throughout the listening experience. From excellent features such as SZA's R&B elements on "telekinesis" to Teezo Touchdown's glam-rock vocals on "modern jam" there is alot for people to enjoy and understand as this is Travis Scott's most experimental album to date. Along with being a great album, the album was a huge success for Travis as it landed him his fourth album to reach number one on Billboard's top 200 chart.


IGOR

Tyler The Creator

Released on May 17, 2019, "Igor" is Tyler The Creator's fifth studio album. The album's genre is listed as Neo-Soul/ Synth Funk/Experimental Hip-Hop. The album is solely produced by Tyler himself and features prominent guest such as Kanye West, Solange, and more. The production is the most standout part of the album. Tyler's use of synths and the cohesive sounding mess leads to beautiful sounding songs such as "Puppet". The album was met with widespread critical acclaim from multiple professional reviewers. The critical acclaim did not just end with reviewers as this album landed Tyler his first grammy for best Rap album of the year for 2020.


The Forever Story

JID

Released on August 26,2022, "The Forever Story" is JID's third studio album. The album's genre is listed as Hip-Hop/ Alternative Hip-Hop. The forever story is a continuation of one of JID's former studio album titled "The Never Story". After waiting for nearly half a decade, the ancipitation that was surrounding the album was intense, but JID did not fail to deliver to fans. The album featured many artist from the Dreamville label which showcased the talent of artists such as EARTHGANG and Ari Lennox.The album was critically aclaimed by professional reviewers as they loved the lyricism and energy JID brought to the album along with its beautiful production.


TA13OO

Denzel Curry

Released on July 27,2018, "TA13OO" is Denzel Curry's third studio album. The album's genre is listed as Hip-Hop, but has heavy influence from metal. "TA13OO" is split into three different parts, the "Light Act","Gray Act", and "Dark Act". As each act transpires, the album starts delving into darker and more sinsiter sounds. The album featured beautiful vocals from Billie Eilish on "Sirens" and high energy from the likes of Zillakami and JPEGMAFIA on "Vengeance". The album was critically acclaimed from both fans and professional reviewers as the experimental sounds, dark lyrical content, and metal influence hooked audiences. The album caused for Denzel to reach greater heights as more eyes were now on him anticipating for his next album.
