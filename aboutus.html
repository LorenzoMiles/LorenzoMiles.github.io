HOW WE WORK



This music review website is run by Alan Rene Martinez and Lorenzo Ramon Miles. Although both of us work together on the lists and albums, usually, the albums ratings are handled by Alan and the lists are handled by Lorenzo. We rate albums on a scale of 1-10. How the album review page works is that on the left side of the screen is the album cover art. To the close right of the art will be the album title in bold followed by the artist/group name in bold below it, which is then folowed by a summary of the album and our opinion on the album. On the right side of the page is a number circled. The circled number is our numerical rating of the album. Below this text is a model representation of the webpage labeled.
